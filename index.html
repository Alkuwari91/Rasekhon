Unit 1: Introduction to Machine Learning (ML)

Machine learning (ML) has become a vital part of our everyday lives. From personalized recommendations while browsing online to assessing eligibility for loans, ML is extensively used in decision-making processes. The availability of big data in real time enhances the learning of ML algorithms, enabling machines to make more accurate and autonomous decisions (Schwab & Zahid, 2020).

Key Topics Covered:

  • The history and evolution of machine learning.
  • Opportunities and challenges of using ML algorithms.
  • The interplay between big data analytics, artificial intelligence (AI), and machine learning.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Understand the role of machine learning in shaping the future of industries.
  • Identify essential skill sets for a career in machine learning.
  • Recognize the challenges of ML adoption and strategies to address them.
